About US

MarauderGames is an association of people who like to learn to make and develop video games in their spare time. This association deals with work in their free time, and no one is pressured into constant work and presence. Plans, problems and ideas in projects are decided in the collective, democratically, and sometimes hierarchically, depending on the need. In case you have an idea, a question, or you want to join the team, please contact us via Discord, or send an email to the members and the team leader.
